Who am I?

In 2018, I graduated with First Class in BSc Computer Science and Multimedia from Queen Mary, University in London. I'm currently working as a software engineer in finance. However, having studied design at Goldsmiths before, I want to combine my creativity with enthusiasm for coding. I am very interested in working with aesthetic designs, games, AR/VR and I spend most of my free time making games in Unity or learning 3D modelling and animation in Blender and Autodesk Maya.

Travelling is another passion of mine. During my time at university I did one year exchange at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, where I met talented people from different backgrounds. After my graduation, I went to Seoul, South Korea for a year, where I was teaching English. I love meeting new people, exploring different cultures, traditions and of course eating local foods.
